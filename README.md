# swe-6-2-tables-and-aggregates-sr

Short response questions for the Creating Tables and Aggregates assignment (Mod 6, Lessons 3–4).

## Setup

Make a draft branch:

```sh
git checkout -b draft
```

Then, add, commit, and push your solution once you are done. Submit a PR to your instructor for feedback.

## Instructions

Answer each question in `short-response.md`. Write in complete sentences. Aim for 3–5 sentences per answer — enough to show you understand the concept, not just that you memorized a definition.

Answer each question in your own words. Be specific — use the exact terms and concepts from the lesson but do so in your own words. Analogies help to illuminate how you personally relate to the concept.

## Grading (18 points)

Your assignment will be graded in two categories:
**Technical Score:** Each of your responses will be evaluated out of 3 points based on the **technical accuracy and precision** of the content.
**Writing Quality Score:** The writing quality of your entire submission will be evaluated out of 3 points.

This assignment is worth 18 points: 3 technical points × 5 questions + 3 writing quality points for the entire assignment.

**ANY SUBMISSION WITH TYPOS, GRAMMAR MISTAKES, AND OTHER DISTRACTING ERRORS WILL BE MARKED INCOMPLETE AND YOU WILL NEED TO RESUBMIT.**
